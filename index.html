Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The kindergarten curriculum focuses on foundational development through play-based and structured activities. Children learn language skills such as recognizing letters, phonics, and simple vocabulary

Elementary School

Elementary education emphasizes academic, social, and personal growth. Language arts include reading comprehension, writing skills, grammar, and vocabulary expansion. Mathematics

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle school curriculum focuses on academic rigor and personal development. Core subjects include advanced language arts with essays and critical analysis, mathematics with algebra and geometry
